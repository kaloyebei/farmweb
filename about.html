Meet the People Behind the Mission

KYIFA is a youth-led, community-driven organization in Kalobeyei, Turkana West, focused on building resilience and long-term opportunity. We strengthen livelihoods through sustainable agriculture, protect the environment, improve water and sanitation, promote gender equality, build peace, support emergency response, and unite communities through sports and culture. We collaborate with local leaders and partners to deliver practical solutions that create meaningful impact.

üéØ Mission

To empower youth and communities in Kalobeyei through sustainable programs that improve livelihoods, protect natural resources, strengthen health and dignity, and promote peaceful coexistence.

üåç Vision

A resilient, inclusive, and self-reliant Kalobeyei where youth lead positive change, communities thrive together, and development is sustainable for future generations.

About KYIFA ‚Äî Common Questions

KYIFA (Kalobeyei Youth Integrated Farmers Association) is a community-led, youth-driven organization working in Kalobeyei and surrounding areas to strengthen livelihoods, improve health and dignity, protect the environment, and promote peace and social cohesion.
Our programs are open to youth, women, vulnerable households, and community members across Kalobeyei and nearby areas, including refugee and host communities. Participation depends on the activity and available spaces.
KYIFA runs eight core programs: Agriculture & Livelihood Development; Environmental Protection & Ecosystem Restoration; Water Sanitation & Hygiene; Watershed Management & Sanitation; Gender Empowerment; Humanitarian Emergency Response; Sports & Cultural Activities; and Peacebuilding & Conflict Mitigation.
You can support KYIFA through partnerships, donations, training support, equipment contributions, volunteering, and joint community projects. We welcome collaboration with NGOs, institutions, local leaders, and well-wishers.
KYIFA operates in Kalobeyei and surrounding areas within Turkana West, collaborating with community groups and partners to deliver practical, local solutions that create long-term impact.
We work through structured community leadership, activity planning, partner coordination, and reporting. We also promote clear communication with stakeholders and responsible use of resources for community benefit.
